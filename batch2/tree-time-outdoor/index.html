Who We Are

Rooted in Barrie.

Tree Time Outdoor Services is a locally owned and operated tree care company based right here in Barrie, Ontario. We're not a franchise — we're your neighbours.

With over 15 years of hands-on experience, our crew brings deep knowledge of the local tree species, soil conditions, and climate patterns unique to Simcoe County. Every job gets the personal attention it deserves — from the first phone call to the final cleanup.

  • Fully licensed, insured & WSIB-covered for your peace of mind
  • Certified arborists on every crew with ongoing safety training
  • Locally owned — we live and work in the Barrie community
