Who We Are

About Superior Grinderz

Superior Grinderz is Barrie’s dedicated stump grinding company. We’re not a general tree service that does stumps on the side — stumps are all we do, and we do them better than anyone.

Founded on the belief that every property deserves a clean, safe, and beautiful landscape, we’ve spent over 15 years perfecting our craft. From tiny ornamental stumps to massive 48-inch hardwoods, our equipment and expertise handle it all.

We serve homeowners, property managers, contractors, and municipalities across Barrie, Innisfil, Orillia, and throughout Simcoe County.
