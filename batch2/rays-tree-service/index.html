Our Story

A Family Tradition of Excellence

"We treat every tree like it's in our own backyard — because this community is our backyard."

Founded in 1998 by Ray Thompson, Ray's Tree Service began with a single chainsaw and a commitment to doing the job right. What started as a one-man operation has grown into Barrie's most trusted tree care company, now run by Ray and his two sons, Mike and Chris.

For over 25 years, we've served homeowners, businesses, and municipalities across Simcoe County with the same values that built our reputation: honesty, hard work, and treating every property with the respect it deserves. We don't just remove trees — we help our neighbours make informed decisions about the health and safety of their landscape.
