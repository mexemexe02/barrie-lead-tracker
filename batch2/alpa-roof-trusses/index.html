Who We Are

Barrie's Premier Roof Truss Manufacturer

For over 20 years, Alpa Roof Trusses has been the backbone of Barrie's construction industry — delivering precision, strength, and reliability to builders across Ontario.

Located at 149 Welham Rd, Barrie, ON L4N 8Y3, our state-of-the-art facility combines cutting-edge CNC fabrication technology with old-world craftsmanship. Every truss that leaves our yard is engineered to exceed Ontario Building Code standards and built to withstand the toughest Canadian winters.

We work directly with homebuilders, general contractors, architects, and property owners — providing end-to-end service from initial design consultation through to on-site delivery and installation. Our in-house engineering team ensures every project receives stamped, permit-ready drawings, saving you time and eliminating rework.

Why Alpa

Why Builders Choose Alpa Roof Trusses

Precision Engineering

Every truss is designed with advanced engineering software and fabricated on CNC equipment for millimeter-perfect accuracy. Stamped drawings included standard.

Premium Materials

We source only SPF #2 & better lumber from certified Canadian mills. Every piece is graded, inspected, and kiln-dried for lasting performance.

On-Time Delivery

Our fleet of boom trucks delivers trusses to your site on schedule — staged and ready for immediate installation. We respect your timeline.

Licensed & Insured

Fully licensed, bonded, and insured. WSIB-compliant crews. All work covered by comprehensive liability insurance — total peace of mind.

Local Expertise

We know Barrie. We understand Ontario frost lines, snow loads, and wind uplift requirements. Our trusses are built for this climate.

Dedicated Support

From estimate to installation, you'll have a dedicated project coordinator. No call centers — just real people on Welham Rd who know your project.
