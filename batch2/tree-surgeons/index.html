Who We Are

The Surgeons
Behind the Saw

Tree Surgeons was founded on a simple principle: trees deserve the same level of care, precision, and expertise as any patient on an operating table. We're not just tree cutters — we're arboricultural clinicians.

Based in Barrie, Ontario, our team brings together ISA Certified Arborists, plant pathologists, and rigging specialists who have trained across Canada and internationally. Every crew member undergoes rigorous safety certification and continuous education in the latest arboricultural science.

We invest in state-of-the-art equipment — from precision rigging systems and crane operations to diagnostic tools that let us assess tree health at the cellular level. When you call Tree Surgeons, you're getting the most advanced tree care available in Simcoe County.
