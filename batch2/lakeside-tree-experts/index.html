What We Do

Complete Tree Care
for Waterfront Properties

We understand the unique challenges of lakefront trees — from erosion control to preserving those priceless shoreline views.

🪓

Tree Removal

Safe, precision tree removal — even in tight waterfront spaces. We handle hazardous trees, storm damage, and deadwood with zero-impact techniques that protect your shoreline.

  • Crane-assisted removal available
  • Emergency storm response 24/7
  • Stump grinding included
  • Shoreline buffer zone compliant
✂️

Pruning & Trimming

Expert structural pruning that improves tree health, enhances your lake view, and reduces storm risk. We follow ANSI A300 standards for every cut.

  • View corridor pruning for lakefronts
  • Deadwood removal & crown thinning
  • Fruit tree & ornamental shaping
  • Young tree structural training
🏥

Tree Health & Disease

Diagnosis and treatment for common Ontario tree diseases. We protect your investment with proactive plant health care programs.

  • Emerald Ash Borer treatment
  • Oak Wilt & Dutch Elm management
  • Soil analysis & deep-root feeding
  • Insect & pest control
🌊

Shoreline Restoration

We specialize in naturalizing and stabilizing lakefront shorelines with native trees, shrubs, and deep-root plantings that prevent erosion.

  • Erosion control plantings
  • Native species selection
  • Riparian buffer restoration
  • LSRCA guideline compliant
🌳

Planting & Landscaping

Strategic tree planting that enhances property value, provides privacy, and thrives in the unique Lake Simcoe microclimate.

  • Large-caliper tree installation
  • Privacy screen & windbreak design
  • Waterfront-appropriate species
  • 3-year establishment guarantee
📋

Arborist Reports

ISA-certified tree assessments for development permits, insurance claims, and property transactions. Detailed, professional reports accepted by all area municipalities.

  • Tree risk assessments
  • Development permit reports
  • Tree preservation plans
  • Insurance & legal documentation

Who We Are

Your Lake Simcoe
Tree Care Partner

Lakeside Tree Experts was born on the shores of Lake Simcoe — founded by a team of arborists who saw that waterfront properties needed a different kind of tree care.

We're not a generic tree service that treats every property the same. We understand lakefront ecosystems: the soil conditions, the wind patterns off the water, the municipal shoreline regulations, and how a single tree can anchor an entire slope against erosion.

Every member of our crew is either an ISA Certified Arborist or working toward certification. We invest in ongoing training, top-tier equipment, and a safety culture that has earned us an impeccable record over 15+ years of operation.
