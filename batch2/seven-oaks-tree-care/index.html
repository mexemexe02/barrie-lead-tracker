What We Do

Comprehensive
Tree Care Services

From routine maintenance to complex urban forestry projects, our certified team approaches every tree with scientific rigor and deep respect for the natural environment.

🪓

Tree Removal

Safe, precision tree removal for hazardous, diseased, or end-of-life trees. Fully insured with advanced rigging and crane-assisted techniques for any location.

✂️

Pruning & Crown Care

ANSI A300-compliant structural pruning, crown thinning, deadwood removal, and vista pruning — every cut made with purpose and tree biology in mind.

🔬

Tree Health Assessments

Comprehensive diagnostic evaluations including soil analysis, pest and disease identification, risk assessment, and customized treatment protocols.

📋

Arborist Reports

ISA-certified tree inventories, pre-construction assessments, tree protection plans, and expert witness services for municipalities, developers, and property owners.

🌱

Planting & Establishment

Species selection, site preparation, and planting with long-term establishment care. We match the right tree to the right place for generations of growth.

⚡

Emergency Response

24/7 emergency storm damage response. Rapid mobilization for fallen trees, hazardous limbs, and property protection across Barrie and surrounding areas.

🪵

Stump Grinding

Complete stump removal and root grinding with minimal landscape disruption. We leave your property clean, level, and ready for replanting or landscaping.

🏗️

Urban Forestry Consulting

Municipal tree canopy planning, development consulting, tree preservation strategies, and urban forest management plans tailored to Ontario regulations.

Who We Are

A Tradition of
Arboricultural Excellence

7 Oaks Tree Care & Urban Forestry Consultants was founded on a simple but powerful principle: every tree deserves care grounded in science, executed with craftsmanship, and driven by genuine environmental stewardship.

Based in Barrie, Ontario, our team brings together ISA Certified Arborists, urban forestry specialists, and experienced tree care professionals who share a deep commitment to preserving and enhancing the urban canopy of Simcoe County.

Whether advising on a municipal tree protection bylaw, conducting a risk assessment on a heritage oak, or carefully pruning a mature maple — we approach every project with the same level of precision, integrity, and respect that our community deserves.
