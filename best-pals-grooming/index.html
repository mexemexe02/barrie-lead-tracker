Who We Are

Caring Hands,
Happy Tails

Best Pals Grooming was born from a simple belief: every pet deserves to feel pampered. Founded in 2018, our Barrie salon has grown into a trusted destination for pet parents across Simcoe County.

Our certified groomers bring years of experience and a genuine love for animals. We use only premium, pet-safe products and stay current with the latest techniques. Whether your best pal is a tiny Yorkie or a majestic Great Dane, they'll receive individualized attention in our calm, welcoming space.
