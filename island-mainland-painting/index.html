About Our Company

Barrie's Trusted Painting Professionals

Island Mainland Painting Co was built on a simple belief: every space deserves to look its absolute best. Founded and operated right here in Barrie, Ontario, we've spent over 15 years transforming homes and businesses across Simcoe County.

From the shores of Kempenfelt Bay to the neighbourhoods of south-end Barrie, our name reflects the breadth of our service — island cottages, mainland homes, downtown storefronts, and everything in between. We take pride in arriving on time, communicating clearly, and leaving every job site cleaner than we found it.
