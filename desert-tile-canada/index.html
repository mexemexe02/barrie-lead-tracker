Our Story

Rooted in Craftsmanship, Driven by Excellence

Desert Tile Canada was born from a passion for transforming ordinary rooms into extraordinary living spaces. Based in Barrie, Ontario, we've spent over a decade and a half perfecting the art of tile installation across Simcoe County.

Our name reflects the warmth, earthiness, and enduring beauty of Mediterranean design — brought home to Canadian interiors. Every project receives meticulous attention, from the first measurement to the final grout line. We don't just install tile; we build foundations for memories.
