Our Story

From a family dream to Thornton's favourite sweet destination.

Thornton Cafe & Ice Cream was born from a simple idea: that every small town deserves a place where families gather, friends reconnect, and everyone leaves with a smile. Nestled in the heart of Thornton, Ontario, we've been serving up handcrafted ice cream and warm hospitality since our doors first opened.

Every batch of our ice cream is churned with care using locally sourced dairy and the finest ingredients we can find. Our waffle cones are baked fresh throughout the day — you'll smell them before you see them. And our coffee? It's the kind that makes you want to stay for a second cup.

Whether you're stopping by after soccer practice, celebrating a birthday, or just treating yourself on a summer afternoon — you're family here at Thornton Cafe.
