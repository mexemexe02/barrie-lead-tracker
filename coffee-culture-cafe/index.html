Our Story

More Than Just
Great Coffee

Coffee Culture Café & Eatery was born from a simple idea: that every neighbourhood deserves a place where people can slow down, connect, and savour something exceptional. Since opening our doors in Barrie, we've been dedicated to sourcing the finest beans, baking from scratch, and creating an atmosphere that feels like a second living room.

Our team is family. From our baristas who remember your order to our chefs who pour heart into every dish, we're here because we love what we do — and we love this community.

  • Locally roasted, ethically sourced coffee beans
  • Scratch kitchen — nothing frozen, nothing fake
  • Community-first: hosting local events & fundraisers
  • Inclusive menu with vegan & gluten-free options
