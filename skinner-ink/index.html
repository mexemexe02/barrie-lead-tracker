The Artist Behind the Needle

Crafting Legends in Ink

Skinner Ink was born in the heart of Barrie with a single mission: elevate tattooing from transaction to transformation. Our lead artist brings over 15 years of experience across studios in Toronto, Vancouver, and internationally — but Barrie is home.

Every piece that leaves this studio carries the weight of that experience. We don't just apply ink — we study your anatomy, respect your skin, and obsess over every detail. From first sketch to final wipe-down, you're in the hands of someone who treats tattooing as the art form it deserves to be.

The Difference

Why Skinner Ink

Choosing the right artist matters. Here's what sets our studio apart from the rest in Barrie and beyond.

Sterile & Safe

Hospital-grade sterilization, single-use needles, and strict health-board compliance. Your safety is non-negotiable.

Award-Winning

Multiple awards at Canadian tattoo conventions. Our work has been recognized for technical excellence and creativity.

Client-First

We listen. Every design starts with your story, your body, your vision. No ego, no rush — just the best ink for you.

Premium Ink

We use only top-tier pigments from Eternal, Intenze, and World Famous — inks that heal beautifully and age gracefully.

Flexible Hours

Evening and weekend appointments available. We work around your schedule because great ink shouldn't wait.

Downtown Barrie

Centrally located with easy parking. Walk in, get inspired, and leave with art you'll carry for a lifetime.

Aftercare Kit

Every client leaves with a complimentary aftercare kit — instructions, balm, and our direct line for any questions.

Lifetime Touch-Ups

We stand behind our work. Complimentary touch-ups on all custom pieces within the first year — because your ink should last.
