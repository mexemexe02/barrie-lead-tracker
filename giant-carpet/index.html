Our Story

Trusted in Simcoe County Since 1998

Giant Carpet opened its doors over 25 years ago with a simple mission: give every customer a beautiful, durable floor at a fair price — and back it with service that turns first-time buyers into lifelong clients.

Today, we're one of the largest independently owned flooring retailers in the Barrie-Bradford region. Our 12,000-square-foot showroom showcases thousands of samples, from budget-friendly laminates to bespoke hardwood and imported area rugs.

We measure success not by square footage sold, but by the number of homeowners and business owners who trust us year after year. When you walk into Giant Carpet, you're family.
